Mission

MissionCode is a platform created to facilitate the connection between students and nonprofits. Our mission is to help college students connect with nonprofits and apply the technical skills they learn in class in helping nonprofits in their cause(s). MissionCode will match the interests that the students have with the interests and social causes of the nonprofits through the resumes that the students submit through the platform.

How It Works

A student will create an account on the web application. After, he or she will have the option to submit his or her resume online, where MissionCode will scan the resume and determine and match any nonprofits that align with the student's interests. If there are nonprofits that match the interests of the student, he or she can contact the nonprofit and voice his or her interest in helping the nonprofit, whether it is to develop a website for them or to create a reservation system. The possibilities are practically endlessly.
